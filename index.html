Before you go

Book two tables

Il Pozzo (Lakones) and Panorama (Afionas) are small, view-table places a long way to drive on spec. Reserve, and ask for a sunset window seat.

Fort now on Wednesday

The 21st is a Tuesday and Angelokastro shuts on Tuesdays — so the fort day has moved to Wednesday the 22nd. It still locks up at 17:30, so aim to be climbing by mid-afternoon.

Wear real shoes

Porto Timoni and Cape Drastis are steep, rocky, unshaded paths. Trainers and water, and save the descents for morning or late afternoon.

It's a driving trip

Days 2–4 are around 9 hours on the road all told. Start by 08:30, and consider merging Days 3 & 4 if you'd rather bank a rest day near base.
